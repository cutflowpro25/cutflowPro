Optimize Your Woodworking Projects

CutFlow Pro is the ultimate tool for carpenters, DIY enthusiasts, and woodworking professionals. Optimize your cut lists, minimize waste, and manage your projects efficiently.

Key Features

Everything you need to work smarter, not harder.

🚀 Intelligent Optimization

Advanced algorithms calculate the absolute most efficient cutting layouts for sheet goods. Save material, save money, save time.

📏 Multi-Unit Support

Seamlessly switch between Metric (mm, cm, m) and Imperial units depending on your specific project needs.

📁 Project Management

Save and organize multiple projects with custom names to keep your entire woodworking workflow structured.

📦 Stock Management

Maintain a library of standard stock sizes for lightning-fast access and easy inventory tracking.

📊 Clear Visual Diagrams

Interactive, easy-to-read cutting diagrams show exact cuts and dimensions to guide your saw work perfectly.

📋 BOM Generation

Automatic Bill of Materials generation ensures you know exactly what materials you need before you start.

📤 Export & Share

Export your cutting plans to CSV or share them directly from the app with colleagues, clients, or your team.

🔒 100% Offline First

All your data is stored locally on your device for absolute privacy and blazing speed. No internet required.
